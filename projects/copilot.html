Overview

As Principal Design Lead, I have worked on concepts and UX designs for the Copilot Researcher Agent in Microsoft 365, including both web and mobile experiences. The patterns and approaches explored through Researcher have also informed other Agents through the broader Agent framework.

In parallel, our team has adopted more AI‑forward ways of working, continuously experimenting with and evaluating new AI tools and capabilities as part of the design process to accelerate exploration, prototyping, and iteration. This shift requires new ways of thinking while still maintaining a strong balance between disciplined product building and AI‑driven experimentation.
